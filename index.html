Thesis

The topic of my thesis is to explore the influence of amthropomorphism toward intelligent customer service. Using questionnaire method to investigate the various levels of anthropomorphism of intelligent customer services, and study whether the higher the degree of anthropomorphism of intelligent customer servicea, the higher the customer satisfaction. Then, use the FB chatbot to manipulate the possible factors of anthropomorphism, and directly monitor the situation through the EEG. Finally, research shows that the more anthropomorphic the intelligent customer services are, the more satisfied customers are and are more willing to continue to use and recommend them to others.
